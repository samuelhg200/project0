Styles


Freestyle

This style is ususally the one most swimmers, casual or not, are usually the most cormftable with. It is ideal for long swims as it allows you to mantain a high speed while exercising most of the muscles in your body!

Backstroke

Finally a stroke you where you can breathe as much as you like to! Take advantage of it! Be careful not to crush your skull against the wall when finishing a lap...

Breastroke

Considered the 'chill' style, you probably have seen multiple 'grandmas' swimming breastroke calmly at your local public swimming pool multiple times. It can be the most relaxing stroke if you want to, however it can also be extremelly demanding in terms of swimming technique, probably the hardest stroke alongside butterfly to master.

Butterfly

Don't misjudge it by its seemingly cute name, only the toughest and strongest are able to domain this style. Ideal for showing off with non-swimmer friends and for short (really short) races. It is recommended for new swimmers to get professional advice when learning to swim butterfly, due to it being the hardest stroke to swim well.
