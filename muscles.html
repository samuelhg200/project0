Muscles


Does swimming really train your whole body?

You probably have hard that swimming trains your whole body like no other sport does. Even though this is not true, swimming is definately one of the most 'complete' sports, when swimming you exercise all type of muscles, upper, lower and even core muscles are put to work!


While swimming you should take advantage of the variety of styles swimming offers if you want to train your body to its full extent. Even if it sounds a bit daunting at the beggining, practicing less commun styles like backstroke and butterfly at early stages of your swimming journey, developing an early habit to vary swim styles will definately help you in the future.
